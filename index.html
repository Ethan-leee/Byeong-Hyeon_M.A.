About Me

I am highly interested in the field of persuasion and social influence in the health communication context. Specifically, My goal is to be an expert on these subjects and make significant contributions to the body of knowledge on persuasion and persuasive campaigns. While be trained in my M.A. program, I wrote M.A. thesis and participated in several research projects, which resulting in one journal article publication and two ICA conference presentations. Furthermore, I am revising a paper to submit to International Journal of Communication.
